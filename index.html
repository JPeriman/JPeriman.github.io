Quick tips

Use a VPN

A VPN encrypts your traffic so snoopers can't read it. Use a reputable paid or university VPN when possible.

Check the network name

Confirm the SSID with staff. Attackers create "evil twin" hotspots with similar names.

Use HTTPS

Look for the lock icon in the address bar. Avoid entering passwords on non-HTTPS pages.

Disable auto-join

Turn off auto-connecting to open networks to avoid accidentally joining malicious networks.

Forget networks

After you leave, forget the network so your device won't auto-reconnect later.

Use mobile data for sensitive tasks

If you are banking or shopping using your phone, use cellular data or use your phone as a hotspot.
